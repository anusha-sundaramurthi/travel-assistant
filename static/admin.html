How to Embed
Add your chatbot to any website in 3 steps.

Step 1 — Create a bot and upload PDFs.
Step 2 — Click Embed Code on the bot card to get your snippet.
Step 3 — Paste it before </body> on your website.

<script src="https://your-app.onrender.com/static/widget.js" data-bot-id="YOUR_BOT_ID" data-api-key="YOUR_API_KEY"> </script>

Your API Key is shown in the sidebar. Keep it private — it's what protects your bot.
